INFINITE

INFINITE is a dance idol group in Korean,who is famous for their orderly and handsome dance.They are my favorite stars, especially their leader Kim Sung Kyu.

In the beginning,they had a hard life after their debut without a good company and much money.And also they had a terrible images initially.So they practiced and practiced and practiced.After getting through many obstacles and working hard for a long time,they finally made it.I think they totally deserve it.

Many people think it's stupid to go after stars,especially k-pop stars,holding the opinion that it is a thing wasting time and money.I don't want to change their thoughts,and I only want to say I get a lot of joy and motivation from them and the process. This kind of feeling can only be attained by people who get through it.
